ABOUT THEM

Established by Felix Notarte Jr. (Jun) and Allan Cabusora in 2014, the Davao City PHP user group began with a modest but impactful meetup attended by 5 individuals, fostering a lasting friendship between Jun and Allan. Through 2017, the community grew with regular meetups. Notably, in October 2017, they engaged in the first DevCon event, and in September 2018, leaders participated in the ADB-AIM Hackathon, initiating a partnership with Sonsystems Software Solution.
